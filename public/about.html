My Background


My name's Brian and I build websites. I'm a San Diego native, mostly. I did move around as a child being a Navy brat. It was hard keeping friends but I kept busy. I always wanted to pull apart my toys to see how they worked and put them back together as a challenge.

I loved playing on the computer and was exposed to coding with simple HTML through the early social media sites. The aspect of computer design was very fascinating. I took a few computer science classes but ended up pursuing my Business degree.

I decided over the past year that I wanted to get back to working with computers and coding was my answer. I took a six month intensive coding bootcamp at UCSD and learned so much and it gave me that same feeling I had when I was growing up of building something from scratch. Growing up I was curious and had a great thirst for knowledge. This sense of building nothing from scratch is very satisfying and I'm happy that I can do this now as a career in Web Development.
